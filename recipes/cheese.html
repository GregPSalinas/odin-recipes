Odin Recipes

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (any type you prefer)
  • 2-3 slices of your favorite cheese (cheddar, American, Swiss, etc.)
  • Butter or margarine
  • Optional add-ins: sliced tomato, cooked bacon, caramelized onions, etc.

Instructions

  1. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat.
  2. Spread a thin layer of butter or margarine on one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Place one slice of bread, butter-side-down, on the skillet.
  4. Layer the cheese slices on top of the bread in the skillet.
  5. If you're adding any optional ingredients, place them on top of the cheese.
  6. Place the second slice of bread on top of the cheese and optional ingredients, butter-side-up.
  7. Cook the sandwich for about 2-3 minutes on each side, or until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted. You can press the sandwich gently with a spatula to help the cheese melt evenly.
  8. Once both sides are toasted and the cheese is melted, remove the sandwich from the skillet.
  9. Let the sandwich cool for a minute or two before cutting it in half.
  10. Serve the grilled cheese sandwich warm and enjoy!
