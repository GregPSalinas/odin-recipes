Odin Recipes

Ingredients

  • Your favorite cereal
  • Milk or milk alternative (dairy-free options like almond milk, soy milk, etc.)

Instructions

  1. Choose Your Cereal: Decide on the type of cereal you'd like to enjoy. There are various options available, including flakes, puffs, granola, oats, and more.
  2. Pour Cereal: Grab a bowl and pour the desired amount of cereal into it. The amount can vary depending on your preference and hunger level.
  3. Add Milk: Pour milk over the cereal. The amount of milk you add can also vary, depending on how creamy or crunchy you want your cereal to be.
  4. Enjoy: With your cereal and milk in the bowl, you're ready to enjoy your breakfast! Grab a spoon and start eating. The cereal will soak up the milk and become softer as you eat.
  5. If you want to enhance the flavor and texture, consider adding toppings like sliced fruits (bananas, strawberries, etc.), chopped nuts (almonds, walnuts, etc.), or even a drizzle of honey.
